Digital Scoreboard

This digital scoreboard tracks the scores of two teams, Team A and Team B, during a game. Users can update the scores in real-time as the game progresses. The scoreboard displays the current scores clearly and provides a final result at the end of the game. Additionally, it includes a reset feature that allows users to clear the scores and start fresh for a new game.
